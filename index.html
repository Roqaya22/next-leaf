Our Sustainable Process

From fallen leaves to valuable products

1

Collection

We gather palm leaves that have fallen naturally, ensuring no trees are harmed in the process.

2

Preparation

The leaves are cleaned and chopped into smaller pieces for processing.

3

Pulp Extraction

We boil the pieces in an eco-friendly solution to extract the natural fibers.

4

Refinement

The fibers are washed and beaten until they become a smooth, workable pulp.

5

Sheet Forming

We mold the pulp into paper sheets using specialized screens.

6

Finishing

The paper is dried, pressed, and smoothed to create high-quality finished products.
